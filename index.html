Introduction

The motivation why this project exists

  • The Situation That Is Happened On This Time

    radio signal technology users are starting to be overloaded. thus experiencing problems to provide a high data rate. the audio transmission using wireless RF requires a pairing time which takes a little time, there is a delay when listening to the audio source, and also results in frequent out of sync between audio and video.

  • VLC for Audio Transmition

    VLC has a high data rate, and abundant bandwidth availability. by utilizing VLC for audio transmission has a very large impact. without requiring additional time for pairing devices, there is no delay, and cheap manufacturing cost

The Objective of This Research

Implement a Visible Light Communication (VLC) system as an alternative to RF and Microwave by utilizing light from an LED light bulb to transmit audio data applications.

  • Hardware Design System

    Design and manufacture special transmitters and receivers for audio transmission with VLC systems

  • Software Design System

    Design and create special algorithms for audio transmission with a maximum frequency of 8000Hz

Problem Definition

  • The Angle Comes The Direction of Light

    Affect the amount of light captured by the receiver

  • The Influence of The Surrounding Environment

    ambient interference in the form of light is very influential on the quality of the data

  • Coverage Distance

    Shord distance in transmitting data

Contributions

the product will be an alternative wireless transmission technology without requiring additional time for pairing devices. in addition to using the basic functions of LEDs as a source of lighting, adding value to the use of LEDs as an audio data transmission medium

The Problem Limitations

  • Data information sent by the transmitter to the receiver is only in the form of audio data

  • The system used only works for one-way communication

  • Communication will not work if there are entities that cannot be penetrated by light

  • This system does not test the security / data transmission panetration

Literature Review

[ Related Work ]

Aims to build a VLC system for audio transmission using 3 room scenarios. The scenario is that the room is dark, ambient and office)

Jeshuthasan et al, Journal IEEE 2018

Purpose to build real-time audio transmission with a visible light communication system with a simple LED. the digital method used successfully reached a distance of 3 meters

Gunjar et al, Journal IEEE 2016

Has conducted research, one of which is to transmit audio data using red LED light bulbs. Data rate achieved by 4Mbps using OOK

Mei et al, Journal IEEE 2018

The digitization process is used to convert analog data from converted audio to digital data so that the LED light carries digital data from the converted audio signal

Sharmila et al, Journal article (2018)

Analog signals from audio are converted into digital data in the form of binary and by using transistor topology switching to control LED on and off

Rekha et al, Journal article (2019)

Product Design

The transmitter has a 3.5mm jack as an audio input and the supply port is a USB port. while the receiver has a 3.5mm jack as the output media that will be supplied to the loudspeaker. the photodioda will be the one with the receiver module

Design System

Transmitter VLC

input can come from smartphones, notebooks, or other media players. microcontroller will change from analog signal to digital data. the output will be passed on the LED

Design System

Receiver VLC

light from the transmitter will be captured by the receiver, then processed by the microcontroller so that it becomes analog audio data again. analog audio data will be connected to the loudspeaker.

Software Architecture

On Transmitter and Reciever VLC

On Transmitter,  input data from the media player will be processed using the existing ADC algorithm on the microcontroller. then going through the data encoding process. On Reciever, digital data in the form of light that has gone through the filtration process will go through the data decoding process. Digital data will be converted to analog using the DAC algorithm
