The Objective of This Research

Applying Visible Light Communication (VLC) system as an alternative system of RF and Microwave by utilizing the light from High Power LED White as communication media to convey data information, one of the examples of data delivered can be audio data.

  • Hardware & Software

    Design and manufacture special transmitters and receivers for voice transmission with VLC systems

  • High Data Rate in Short Distance

    the system will be made with a high data rate at a low distance of one meter

  • Low Cost for Production

    The product to be manufactured will use minimal costs but still be eligible for use

The Scope of Research

  • Data information sent by the transmitter to the receiver is only in the form of audio data

  • The system used only works for one-way communication

  • Communication will not work if there are entities that cannot be penetrated by light

  • This system does not test the security / data transmission panetration

Literature Review

VLC is a data communication part of optical communication technology that uses visible light between 400 and 800 Thz (780-375 nm)

Wikipedia, May 2020

Ancient China used fire to send information from the guards' offices to the Great Wall, then another example was the lighthouse on each coast to navigate cargo ships

Zhaocheng Wang, Qi Wang, Wei Huang, Zhengyuan Xu, Journal Article (2018)

Visible light has certain advantages over traditional radio frequency as a medium for communication

A. Jovicic, J. Li and T. Richardson, Journal & Magazine (2013)

Analog data from the audio is connected directly to the LED so that the light emitted by the LED carries analog data

Bolli Jagadeeswari, Charapu Sai Anusha, Dangeti Monisa, Medisetti Preethi, Journal article (2019)

The digitization process is used to convert analog data from converted audio to digital data so that the LED light carries digital data from the converted audio signal

M. Sharmila, M. Shrin Shifana, V. Theebica, V. Sangeethapriya, M. Prathiba, Journal article (2018)

Analog signals from audio are converted into digital data in the form of binary and by using transistor topology switching to control LED on and off

Rekha R , Priyadarshini C , Pooja R , R Prashanth, Suma V Shetty, Journal article (2019)

Product Design

The transmitter has a 3.5mm jack as an audio input and the supply port is a USB port. while the receiver has a 3.5mm jack as the output media that will be supplied to the loudspeaker. the photodioda will be the one with the receiver module

Design System

Transmitter VLC

input can come from smartphones, notebooks, or other media players. microcontroller will change from analog signal to digital data. the output will be passed on the LED

Design System

Receiver VLC

light from the transmitter will be captured by the receiver, then processed by the microcontroller so that it becomes analog audio data again. analog audio data will be connected to the loudspeaker.

Software Architecture

On Transmitter and Reciever VLC

On Transmitter,  input data from the media player will be processed using the existing ADC algorithm on the microcontroller. then going through the data encoding process. On Reciever, digital data in the form of light that has gone through the filtration process will go through the data decoding process. Digital data will be converted to analog using the DAC algorithm
